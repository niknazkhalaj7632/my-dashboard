Frequently Asked Questions
When are commissions paid?›
Commissions are calculated monthly and paid within 5 business days after month-end. You'll receive an email confirmation when the payment is processed.
How do I track my referrals?›
All referrals are tracked via your unique referral links. You can view real-time stats on the Referral Links page, including clicks, sign-ups, and funded accounts.
What is the Sub-IB override rate?›
You earn a 6% override on all commissions generated by your sub-IB partners. This is automatically calculated and included in your monthly commission report.
How do I add a new referral link?›
Go to the Referral Links page and click "+ New Link". You can name each link for tracking purposes (e.g. by campaign or platform).
What payment methods are available?›
We support bank wire transfer, USDT (TRC-20 and ERC-20), and internal wallet transfer. Payment method can be changed in Account Settings.
